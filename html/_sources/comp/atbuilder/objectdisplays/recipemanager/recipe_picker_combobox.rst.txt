Recipe Picker (Small)
#####################

Display which allows selecting a recipe with a combobox and setting as active. See also chapter :ref:`atvise visualization > Recipe management - Activate recipes<recipe_activate>`.

.. rubric:: Parameters
    
General:

- **config node** -- Node for configuration of templates and recipes (Default: AGENT.OBJECTS.recipes)
- **template name** -- Name of the template for a prefiltered view 

Appearance:

- **fill color** -- Fill color (Default: SYSTEM.GLOBALS.atvFillColor)
