Warning
#######

In a redundancy environment this dialog shows warning and information dialogs corresponding to the current redundancy status. It refers to the "message_dialog" display in the folder "dialogs" of the corresponding layout.

These are the potential messages:

- Redundancy status has changed. Reload page to apply potential changes.
- Redundancy status has changed. You are now connected to SNOP.
- Redundancy status has changed. You are now connected to the active server.

.. rubric:: Parameter

- :advstyle:`Advanced` **trigger on button 1** -- Being triggered on click on button 1
- :advstyle:`Advanced` **trigger on button 2** -- Being triggered on click on button 2
