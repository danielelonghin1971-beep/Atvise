.. _ua_overview:

Introduction
##############

:ref:`UaNode functions<uanode_functions>` are deprecated and replaced by :ref:`Ua functions<ua_functions>` starting with atvise 3.6. The functions can still be used to provide backward compatibility, however, it may result in errors and/or undefined behavior in conjunction with :ref:`access control<access_control_intro>`.
