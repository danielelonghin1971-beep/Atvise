First steps: atvise scada
#########################

The main goal of this tutorial is to familiarize you with the engineering process of a web visualization using atvise with a scada license. In small steps and using specific examples, you will learn how essential tools and interfaces work.

In the first step you will learn how to import data variables, how to draw a simple display and how to generate and view the visualization.

After this we will take a look at the dynamization of elements, including the four ways of addressing a data variable.
